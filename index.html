    About The Church

    Our Community

    The unity and diversity within the church enriches the church community, equipping people in the church for righteousness to glorify Christ where we can socialize and support each other, feel a sense of belonging, and build a support network that you can rely on.

    Church Mission

    To reach and influence the world by building a large Christ-centred, Bible-based church, changing mindsets and empowering people to lead and impact in every sphere of life. Facilitating this communal growth, worship, and prayer is part of the mission of the church, too.
